Instructions

Help Thomas the Turtle get to the flag!

  • To move Thomas, use arrow keys or WASD.
  • Press H to hide/show the instructions and controls.
  • To generate a new maze, press G or click the Generate Maze button. Optionally, specify the number of rows and columns in the input boxes.
  • You can do it! But if you can't, press L or click the Show Solution button.
